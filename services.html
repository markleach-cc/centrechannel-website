How I Work

I solve problems.

You've got a board demanding digital growth. An agency that's stopped delivering. A replatforming project that's going sideways. That's when you call me.

01

It starts with a conversation.

A 30-minute call. No pitch, no slides. I want to understand your situation: where you are, what's stuck, and what good looks like.

Sometimes that call is all you need. A fresh perspective, a recommendation, a contact. If that's the case, we shake hands and you're on your way.

02

If there's more to untangle, we diagnose.

Most clients don't know exactly what's broken. They know something isn't working. Revenue is flat. Teams are frustrated. Agencies are pointing fingers.

I spend 10 days inside your business. Interrogating the data, the tech stack, the team dynamic. Not to produce a deck, but to isolate the root cause.

You get a clear answer: here's what's actually wrong, and here's what to do about it.

03

Then we decide what happens next.

Sometimes the diagnosis is enough. You take the findings to your team or your agency and run with it.

Sometimes you need someone to stay. To govern the roadmap, manage the agencies, translate between the board and the engineers. To make sure what we found actually gets fixed.

That's when I embed as a fractional director or lead the programme myself.
